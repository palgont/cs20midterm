Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the process of buying a home?
To start the home-buying process, first determine your budget, get pre-approved for a mortgage, and connect with one of our real estate agents to explore available properties.
Do I need a real estate agent to buy a house?
While you can buy a house on your own, having a real estate agent simplifies the process and helps you find the best deals, negotiate prices, and handle legal paperwork.
What are the common costs when purchasing a house?
Common costs include the down payment, closing costs, home inspection fees, and potential renovation expenses.
How long does it take to buy a home?
On average, it takes 30 to 60 days to close on a home after making an offer, depending on financing, inspections, and negotiations.
Can I buy a house with a low credit score?
Yes, but options may be limited. You may qualify for FHA loans or other programs that help buyers with lower credit scores.
